Research

Measure flows, inverse problems, and scientific machine learning.

My current research develops mathematical and computational tools for working with probability measures, especially in settings where the geometry of the problem matters.

  • Measure-theoretic approaches to scientific machine learning
  • Inverse problems, sampling, and uncertainty quantification
  • Bayes Hilbert spaces and measure flows
  • Semialgebraic neural networks and representation theory
